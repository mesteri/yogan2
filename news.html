About us

Our packing is eco-friendly. For packing we use bioplastics. Bioplastics are basically plastics made from plants. The starch within most plants can be used to form plastic polymers. Our bioplastic made from corn and the name of the polymer is Polylactic Acid or PLA for short.

We are a small vegan yoghurt manufacture in Plymouth. Our products are exclusively of vegetable origin. For our youghurs we use coconut, almond and soy milk. You can find wide choices of flavors with us.
